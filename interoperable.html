Building an animatronic bird

During this weeks lecture 17.02.2026, we build a small animatronic bird as a group.

Project Overview

Making my building block interoperable

Since I want my airplane to be more than just a glider, I equip it with another tinkering material, electronics, or in other words, a flight controller, 2 servos and a motor, as well as a battery.

Material

Here one can see again the material I used.

Material

To connect all the electronic component together I pulled out my soldering iron my schematic and went to work. After soldering and not burning myself I was ready to test out the motor. I had to stop testing it indoors because it was way too loud and making way too much wind. But it worked and the plane will fly for sure.

Material

I have a 4s LiPo battery, it doesnt fit into the airplane, so I opened up the battery (carefully) and separated the 4 Cells in to a pair of two cells.

Building Block

I opted for 3D printing my building block since I had a lot of difficult geometries in it. After printing, I fitted the motor in the back and the battery in the front, with two cells left and two cells right. Here not on the picture is the rest of the electronics.

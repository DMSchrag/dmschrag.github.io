What are some good properties

For once when I started tinkering more it hit me like a truck that stuff costs money, especially if you want quality. I would really like to use Maxon DC Motors but they are just way more expensive than what I could get on Aliexpress. The availability of Datasheets and Instructions are also needed if you go more into electronics hardware, I can't probably use a nice microcontroller if there is absolutely no documentation about it. It hasn't happened to me yet but I have already purchased some small knock off microcontrollers which were really difficult to set up since documentation was lacking. My parts when I build something needs to be interoperable, when I tinkered on my commuter bike really wanting to mount that basket on my bike, there was no other choice then to design and build a mount on my own. I could have bought a nice bike basket but that wouldnt have fit with my expectations. Also a cheap basket and 3D printing a bit of plastic is quite cheaper than buying one off the shelve.

Building an animatronic bird

During this weeks lecture 17.02.2026, we build a small animatronic bird as a group.

Project Overview

Bird Head

We got some carton, hot glue and googly eyes as well as some puffles to make a really nice bird head out of those materials, it really gives the whole bird a pit of a persona.

Bird Body

To actually make it animatronic we had 3 servos in the body, two to move the wings left and right and one for the head.

Finished bird

This would be the finished bird, ad one can see he has a lot of sass, an an unresistable charm!
